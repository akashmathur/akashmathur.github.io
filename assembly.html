Lego Robotic Arm

January 2018 - April 2018

Description

  • Created a Multi-Degree-of-Freedom Robotic Arm prototype with multiple functionalities including 360° lateral & vertical rotations

  • Developed with NIOS II Assembly Programming via. Lego Controller & DE1-SoC Board

  • Used GPIO pins, sensor devices, 375 RPM motors and 1420 RPM motors

  • Thorough use of interrupts, interrupt handlers, subroutines, timer devices, and instruction encoding
